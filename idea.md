# App Idea: Avoid Social Media

## Core Mission
To break the "autopilot" scrolling cycle by using the most powerful psychological trigger available: **The user's own voice.**

## Key Unique Features
1. **Personalized Pattern Interrupters:** Instead of generic system beeps, the app plays voice notes recorded by the user (or loved ones) to remind them of their goals.
2. **Escalation Strategy:** Logic-based reminders that evolve as the user continues to scroll:
   - **Level 1 (Gentle):** A soft reminder to check in with reality.
   - **Level 2 (Firm):** A direct instruction to put the phone down.
   - **Level 3 (Urgent):** Persistent, high-intensity interventions.
3. **Total Distraction Budget:** A collective time limit across all "wasted-time" apps, preventing users from simply jumping from one social app to another.
4. **Privacy First Architecture:**
   - **Zero Cloud:** No accounts, no servers, no data collection.
   - **Local Storage:** All voice recordings and usage stats never leave the device.
   - **Anonymous:** No tracking of who the user is.

## Target Audience
People who find themselves scrolling social media unconsciously and want a "digital tap on the shoulder" to regain control of their attention.
